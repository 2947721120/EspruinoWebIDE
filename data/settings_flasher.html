NOTE: This will only flash devices with the Espruino USB Bootloader (like the Espruino Board).

To use this, please do the following:

  • If connected, click the disconnect button
  • Click the connect button, and ensure that the firmware URL below updates correctly (or fill it in manually - see http://www.espruino.com/Troubleshooting)
  • Click the disconnect button
  • Put the board into bootloader mode by holding down the BTN1 button on the board while pressing and releasing RST. The blue LED should start glowing.
  • Click the connect button in the Web IDE again
  • Click 'Write Firmware' and wait. Flashing can take around 5 minutes
  • When finished, the status in the top left will change and you can disconnect and press the reset button on Espruino to start using it again.
  • If flashing fails, just repeat all but the first step.
